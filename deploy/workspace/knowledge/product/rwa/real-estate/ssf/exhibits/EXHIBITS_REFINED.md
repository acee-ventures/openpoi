# SSF Refined Exhibits (A & E)

This document provides the high-signal, clinically refined contract clauses for the Property Owner's review. These clauses prioritize **mandatory reporting**, **order of priority**, and **audit rights**.

---

## Exhibit A: Proceeds Waterfall & Custody Controls

### A1. Direct On-Chain Split

Every unit of Purchase Proceeds (1,000 USDC per SSFShareToken) shall be automatically split upon transaction confirmation:

- **P1: Reserve Vault (50 USDC / 5%)**: Reserve Redemption Feature (non-guaranteed exit mechanism).
- **P2: Owner Proceeds (450 USDC / 45%)**: T+0 settlement to Owner.
- **P3: SPV Controlled Vault (500 USDC / 50%)**: Operations + incentive budget, 2-of-2 multisig (ACEE + OR).

### A2. Priority Disbursement

Funds from the SPV Controlled Vault (P3) are disbursed with ACEE + OR dual-signature:

1. **ACEE Fee**: Tokenization service fees as defined in Schedule 1.
2. **Operational Costs**: SPV operational expenses.
3. **Incentive Budget**: Discretionary incentive allocation, non-guaranteed.

### A3. Information Rights (Pre-Settlement)

- **Weekly Sales Update**: OR shall receive a summary of new whitelisted buyers and total share volume.
- **Registry Access**: OR shall have continuous read-only access to the Buyer Registry (Entity ID, Jurisdiction, Share count).

### A4. Owner Settlement & Evidence

- **Settlement**: T+0 on-chain (P2 Owner Proceeds settled immediately upon purchase).
- **Reporting Obligation**: Every disbursement must be accompanied by a **Distribution Report** delivered to the OR.

### A5. OR Consent Gates (Reserved Matters)

Prior written consent from the **Owner Representative (OR)** is required for:

1. **Whitelist Modifications**: Any change to the Owner's payout address or the Manager's fee destination.
2. **Economic Logic**: Any change to Issue Price, Floor Price, or Waterfall Priority.
3. **Fee Schedule**: Any modification to the ACEE Fee structure defined in Priority 1.
4. **Frequency Changes**: Any modification to the Settlement Cycle or Payment Deadline.
5. **Rebate Pool Cap Override**: Any expenditure exceeding the 40% cap defined in E3.

## Exhibit E: Buyer Transparency & Rebate Program

### E1. Mandatory Buyer Registry

The Manager shall maintain a real-time **Buyer Registry** accessible to the Owner via a secured Data Room. The Registry shall include:

- **Entity Identification**: Buyer ID, Wallet Address, and Jurisdiction.
- **Compliance Status**: Verified accredited status (Reg D 506(b) vs. 506(c)), KYC/AML approval date.
- **Transaction History**: Purchase timestamp, Share count, and $POI Reward accrual history.

### E2. Data Room Access & Audit Rights

- **Access Control**: Owner-designated Compliance Officers or Auditors shall have read-only access to PII-level evidence (Accreditation proofs) within the secure Data Room.
- **Audit Right**: Owner reserves the right to audit the Buyer Registry against on-chain transactions on a quarterly basis.

### E3. Discretionary Rebate Policy

- **Nature of Rebate**: Any rebate or incentive program is **Discretionary** and does not constitute a contractual right of the Buyer.
- **Funding Cap**: The Rebate Pool shall not exceed **40%** of the Treasury Allocation. **Any override of this cap requires prior written consent from the OR.**
- **Prohibited Marketing**: No rebate shall be marketed as a "Guaranteed Yield" or "Profit Share".
